## **[5.0.5]**

### **Release Notes**

This update refines metrics reporting for the `onReady` event and fixes a slight audio delay in MOQ playback that could gradually become noticeable after repeated stream switches.

### **Changelog**

### Fixed

- slight audio delay in MOQ playback that could gradually become noticeable after repeated stream switches

### Improved

- metrics for `onReady` event
